# Privacy Policy for PayPal Calc

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026  
**Effective Date:** January 18, 2026

## Introduction

PayPal Calc ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

PayPal Calc is a fee calculator tool that helps users calculate PayPal transaction fees. This app is developed and maintained independently and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to PayPal, Inc.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not use the application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Automatically Collected

When you use PayPal Calc, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns through third-party services:

#### Google Firebase Analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. This service may collect:
- Device information (model, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
- Usage data (features used, time spent in app, navigation patterns)
- App performance data (crashes, errors, response times)
- General location data (country/region level only, derived from IP address)

#### Google AdMob
We use AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect:
- Device information and identifiers
- Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
- Approximate location (for ad targeting purposes)
- IP address

### Information You Provide

**User Calculations:**
- The amounts you enter for fee calculations
- Currency selections
- Custom fee configurations

**Important:** All calculation data you enter (amounts, currencies, custom fees) is stored ONLY on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your calculation data on our servers. Your financial information never leaves your device.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the automatically collected information to:

1. **Improve the App**
   - Understand which features are most used
   - Identify and fix bugs and crashes
   - Optimize performance and user experience

2. **Analytics and Statistics**
   - Analyze usage trends and patterns
   - Make informed decisions about future features
   - Monitor app health and stability

3. **Advertising**
   - Display relevant advertisements
   - Measure ad performance
   - Generate revenue to support free app availability

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Your calculation data (amounts, currencies, custom fees) is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences
- This data is NOT transmitted to any servers
- This data is only accessible by the app on your device
- Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

### Third-Party Services
Data collected by Firebase Analytics and AdMob is stored on Google's servers and is subject to Google's security practices and privacy policies.

### Security Measures
While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. The app uses industry-standard security measures provided by the Android platform and Google services.

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
- **Local Data:** You can clear your calculation history and custom settings by clearing the app's data through Android settings
- **Analytics:** You cannot directly access analytics data as it is aggregated and anonymized
- **Advertising:** You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

### Advertising Opt-Out
To opt out of personalized advertising:
- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- Or visit: [https://www.google.com/settings/ads](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)

### Data Deletion
To delete all your data:
1. **Local Data:** Uninstall the app or clear app data in Android settings
2. **Analytics Data:** Analytics data is automatically deleted after a retention period set by Google
3. **Contact Us:** For specific data deletion requests, contact us at the email below
